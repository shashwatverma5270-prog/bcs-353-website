Safety Features

Your security is our priority, featuring advanced driver assistance systems.

Adaptive Cruise Control

Maintains a safe distance from the car ahead, automatically adjusting speed.

Emergency Brake Assist

Automatically applies brakes to prevent or mitigate collisions in emergency situations.

Adaptive Headlights

Adjusts the beam pattern based on road conditions and traffic, enhancing visibility at night.

Rearview Camera & Parking Sensors

Provides a clear view of what’s behind and alerts the driver to obstacles while parking.

Performance Features

Engineered for precise handling and enhanced stability under challenging conditions.

Torque Vectoring

Distributes torque to individual wheels for precise handling and enhanced cornering performance.

All-wheel Drive (AWD)

Delivers power to all four wheels for enhanced traction and stability.

eLSD

The Electronic Limited Slip Differential distributes power to wheels with better traction, improving handling.

Drive Modes

Allows drivers to choose from different driving modes, such as Eco, Sport, and Comfort.
